Coachwise is a mobile app for personal trainers and gyms to deliver fitness programs to clients. To transition the app from an internal tool to a white label product, the redesign of the client facing side implements a refreshed sitemap, new branding elements and increased functionality.

Qualitative research methods were utilised to gather data for two different audiences and inform decisions for added functions. The app draws inspiration from existing competitors in navigation, layout and behaviour. However, unlike the emphasis on strength that others display, the app puts the values of JPS first. Deliverables included UX/UI research outcomes and proposals, a one-page style guide and app mockups.

Images top to bottom, left to right

1 Home page of the app in light and dark versions on the two left screens. Users are able to quickly start their next workout via the "Start" button on the main screen. It leads them to the exercise page pictured on the right screen, where users log their sets and workout. The workout log is intended to be no-scroll, meaning that personal notes about the exercise can be viewed without another touch.

2 The notifications, settings and profile pages. These house alerts and direct messages with the coach, personal details and app settings, and the client's workout journey, recorded through graphs, numbers and photos.

3 The navigation of the workout days which lay out what exercises are to be completed. The symbols next to each day change depending on whether the sets are complete, incomplete, or in progress. Access to past and current weekly/monthly programs can be found in the hamburger menu.

4 The past and current workout programs and an example of a popup/modal box design. The workout directory uses similar symbols to convey inactive and active programs.

5 The library of all exercises, individual exercise details and statistical history of any completed sets.

6 Refreshed sitemap to consider ease of use and practicality. Tools like the load calculator are placed on the same screen as the exercise page because it impacts how the workout will be performed.

7 Branding elements for Coachwise. JPS are rooted in science-backed programs, education and enjoyment of being active. Hence the colour scheme is clean and calm yet inviting and encouraging. Font choices, icons and colour application are also minimal to allow for legibility and simplicity in a dense app.
