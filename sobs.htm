I'm Sick Of Being Sore is a lighthearted read to help those in pain understand their bodies. Author, Matthew Green, wanted a minimal book design to comfort and ease readers.

By adapting an unconventional layout, the design keeps readers engaged. Witty illustrations and a soothing colour palette also supports the story by encouraging an easy read. To promote the book and collect an email subscription list, a Wix website↗ was developed using the same brand elements.
